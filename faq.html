FAQs

Getting Financial Aid for Medicine

I still haven't gotten financial aid. When will I receive it?
Sorry about that. You will receive it as soon as we get the necessary amount of donations. We may currently be short on donations. If financial aid is urgent, please contact support@giftcure.org and we will look into what we can do.
How do I withdraw my request for financial aid?
Just shoot us an email to support@giftcure.org. We'll take care of it.
What if I need nonprescription medication? How do I apply for financial aid?
Sorry, we do not provide financial aid for nonprescription medication for the sole reason that we can't confirm if the money is actually used for buying medications.
Who is eligible for medication assistance from Gift Cure?
To be eligible, patients must have a valid prescription and a valid P2P account for receiving funds. Also the recipient must be under 18, but the one who fills out the application must be above 18. Exceptions to this eligibility criteria may be given if email is sent to support@giftcure.org.
Are there any income restrictions to qualify?
We don't list specific income restrictions. However, Gift Cure prioritizes assisting those with the greatest financial need.
How long does it take to process an application?
Processing times can vary, but we strive to review applications as quickly as possible. You will receive updates on the status of your application via email.
What if I don't have a P2P account?
Please contact us at support@giftcure.org and we will figure something out.

Donating Money / Volunteering

What is the minimum amount of money I can donate?
A minimum of $5 will be helpful. If you can provide more than that, please do!
Can I donate in someone else's name?
Yes, you can donate in honor or memory of someone special. Please let us know during the donation process.
How can I volunteer with Gift Cure?
We have a variety of volunteer opportunities available! You can learn more about volunteering on our website or by joining our Discord server (discord.giftcure.org).
Do I need any specific skills to volunteer?
We value all skillsets! Whether you have experience in fundraising, finance, translation, or simply want to lend a helping hand, we have a place for you.
Can I join a volunteer board?
Yes, Gift Cure is powered by dedicated volunteers who lead our various boards. Please visit our Discord server (discord.giftcure.org) for more information about board opportunities.

More Questions?
